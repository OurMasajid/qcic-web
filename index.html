Not Just a Mosque for Muslims — A Home for the Whole Neighborhood

QCIC belongs to Leander as much as it belongs to its congregation. Neighbors of every background are welcome to walk through our doors — to observe a prayer, ask a question, receive free medical care, or share a meal with us under the string lights on our lawn.

Always Open, No Appointment Needed

Stop by any time prayers are held. Sit in the back, watch, and ask us anything — no dress code to worry about, no expectation to participate.

Free Health Clinic for Everyone

Our volunteer clinic offers basic medical care at no cost to uninsured and underinsured neighbors in Leander — regardless of faith or background.

Interfaith Gatherings on Our Lawn

Each year we open our lawn for an Interfaith Eid Dinner, inviting neighbors of every faith to break bread and celebrate together under the lights.

Tours & School Visits Welcome

Students, scout troops, and curious neighbors can schedule a guided tour and learn what actually happens inside a masjid.

Community Life

Events & Activities for Every Season

From nightly Ramadan iftars to summer camp for the kids, QCIC's calendar stays full — and everyone in the community is invited, not just members.

Weekly

Jumuʻah Congregation

Friday khuṭbah and ṣalāh open to the whole community — first-time visitors are always welcome to sit in and observe.

Seasonal

Ramadan Nights

Nightly Taraweeh prayers, with a shared community Iftar every Friday and Saturday throughout the blessed month.

Annual

Interfaith Eid Dinner

A lawn dinner under string lights where neighbors of every faith celebrate Eid together as one community.

Annual

ATX Eid Fest

Austin's united Eid celebration — a bazaar, halal food, and family activities alongside masajid from across the region.

Seasonal

Family Night Under the Stars

A post-Taraweeh family gathering with suhoor and lawn camping for the kids — weather permitting, one tent per family.

Annual

QCIC Summer Camp

A month of Qurʾān, tajwīd, and Islamic studies mixed with chess, storytelling, and games for ages 6–18.

Monthly

Noor Kids Storytime

Storytelling and youth workshops on faith and character, paired with a community potluck for the whole family.

Weekly

Quranic Arabic Course

A Sunday program teaching adults the Arabic vocabulary needed to understand the Qurʾān directly.

Who We Are

A Multi-Ethnic, Wide-Open Community of Faith

QCIC is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization guided by the teachings of the Qurʾān and the example of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. We are a multi-ethnic, multi-racial, multi-lingual, non-sectarian community — and our doors swing open just as wide for our Leander neighbors as they do for our own congregation.

  • Daily congregational prayers and Friday Jumuʻah
  • Year-round programs for children, youth, and families
  • Free health clinic and counseling open to the whole community
  • Interfaith friendship, tours, and civic engagement with our neighbors

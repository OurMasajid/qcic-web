Support Our Masjid

Your Generosity Sustains This Community

Every prayer held, every child taught, every patient seen at our free clinic is made possible by gifts from people like you.

Where Your Gift Goes

Every Dollar Stays in the Community

QCIC is run almost entirely by volunteers, which means the vast majority of what's donated goes directly toward keeping our doors open and our programs running.

  • Daily operations, utilities, and building upkeep
  • Quran Academy classes and youth programs
  • Supplies and staffing for our free health clinic
  • Community events, outreach, and interfaith gatherings
Ways to Give

Choose How You'd Like to Support Us

All gifts are welcome, whatever form they take.

Zakāh

Fulfill your obligatory annual Zakāh — we can help calculate eligible categories of giving.

Sadaqah

General voluntary giving that goes toward wherever the community's needs are greatest.

Debt-Free Campaign

Help us pay down the Qard Hasanah loan on our building, interest-free, ahead of schedule.

Sponsor an Event

Cover the cost of an Iftar, the Interfaith Eid Dinner, or Summer Camp for a family in need.

Free Health Clinic

Compassionate Care for Every Neighbor

Basic medical care at no cost to uninsured and underinsured neighbors in Leander — regardless of faith or background.

Why We Do This

Healthcare Shouldn't Depend on What You Can Afford

Our volunteer clinic was started so that no neighbor in Leander has to go without basic care simply because they lack insurance. Licensed volunteer doctors, nurses, and support staff donate their time to see patients — Muslim or not, member or not, at no charge.

The clinic is one expression of a simple belief that runs through everything QCIC does: caring for our neighbors is part of our faith, not separate from it.

What the Clinic Offers

Our Services

General Care

Check-Ups & Basic Screenings

Blood pressure and blood sugar checks, general physical exams, and basic health screenings for adults who don't have regular access to a doctor.

Common Illness Care

Colds, Flu & Everyday Ailments

Walk-in care for common illnesses, along with guidance on managing chronic conditions and referrals to specialists or low-cost pharmacies when needed.

This clinic is not for medical emergencies. If you or someone else is experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room. Clinic days, hours, and walk-in availability vary — call the office ahead of your visit to confirm.

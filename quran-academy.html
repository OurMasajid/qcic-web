Quran Academy · Maktab

Learn to Read, Recite, and Live the Qurʾān

A Maktab program for children and adults — from first letters to full memorization — taught with patience, one student at a time.

About the Academy

A Class for Every Stage of Learning

Our Maktab program meets students exactly where they are — whether that's a child sounding out the Arabic alphabet for the first time, a teenager working toward memorizing (ḥifẇ) the Qurʾān, or an adult learning to recite correctly as an act of worship later in life.

Small class sizes, qualified teachers, and a one-on-one recitation ('sabaq) model mean every student gets individual correction and encouragement, not just a lecture.

  • Noorani Qaida for absolute beginners
  • Tajwīd and fluent recitation coaching
  • Ḥifẇ (memorization) track for committed students
  • Qurʾānic Arabic for adults, Sunday mornings
Class Tracks

Find the Right Starting Point

Every student begins with a short placement conversation so we can recommend the right track.

Noorani Qaida

Arabic letters, sounds, and basic reading rules for young beginners.

Tajwīd & Recitation

Rules of correct pronunciation, applied verse by verse with a teacher.

Ḥifẇ Program

A structured memorization track with regular revision and testing.

Adult Arabic Class

Sunday mornings — Qurʾānic vocabulary and grammar for grown-up beginners.

Class days and times vary by term and age group. Call or email the office for the current schedule, tuition, and to enroll a new student — we're happy to walk you through it.
